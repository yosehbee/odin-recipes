Pizza recipe

This recipe will tell you how to make a fire pizza wooo

Ingredients

  • water
  • flour
  • pizza sauce
  • olive oil
  • mozzerella cheese
  • basil

Steps

  1. Mix water and flour and salt
  2. Knead and let rest
  3. Gest rested dough and roll out into circle
  4. Spread sauce evenly across dough
  5. Spread cheese across sauce
  6. Bake for 1 minute in your thousand degree brick oven
